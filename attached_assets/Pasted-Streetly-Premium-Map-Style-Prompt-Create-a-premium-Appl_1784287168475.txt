Streetly Premium Map Style Prompt

Create a premium Apple-inspired navigation map for Streetly that matches the visual quality of Uber, Bolt, Lyft, and Apple Maps.

Use the Mapbox Navigation Day style as the foundation:

mapbox://styles/mapbox/navigation-day-v1

Design Requirements

* Bright, clean, minimalist appearance.
* Soft off-white land background.
* Light gray secondary roads.
* Slightly darker major roads.
* Green parks and vegetation with subtle saturation.
* Light blue lakes and rivers.
* Minimal map labels with modern typography.
* No excessive road shields or visual clutter.
* Smooth anti-aliased vector rendering.
* Rounded road edges.
* High-resolution vector tiles.
* Retina/4K optimized.
* Flat modern design with no harsh colors.
* Premium navigation appearance similar to Apple Maps.

Route Styling

* Main route: Solid black (#111111).
* White outer casing (4–6 px).
* Rounded line caps and joins.
* Smooth animated drawing when route is calculated.
* Subtle shadow beneath the route for depth.
* Animated glow while recalculating.

Pickup Marker

* Black circular pin.
* White center dot.
* Soft drop shadow.
* Smooth drop animation.

Destination Marker

* Purple circular pin.
* White center.
* Gentle bounce animation.

User Location

* Bright blue pulsing location dot.
* Semi-transparent accuracy circle.
* Direction arrow rotates with heading.
* Smooth location updates.

Driver Vehicles

* Custom Streetly vehicle icons.
* Rotate according to travel direction.
* Smooth interpolation between GPS updates.
* No snapping or jitter.
* Soft shadow beneath each vehicle.

Camera

* Smooth zoom transitions.
* Animated pan.
* Navigation mode tilts camera to approximately 60°.
* Auto-rotate in travel direction.
* Ease-in/ease-out animations.

UI Theme

* Floating glassmorphism controls.
* Frosted white panels.
* Rounded corners (20–28 px).
* Soft shadows.
* Apple-style typography.
* Minimalist interface with generous spacing.

Dark Mode

Automatically switch to:

mapbox://styles/mapbox/navigation-night-v1

Maintain the same premium design language with deep charcoal backgrounds, muted roads, and high-contrast route lines.

Performance

* GPU-accelerated vector rendering.
* 60 FPS animations.
* Smooth pinch, rotate, tilt, and zoom gestures.
* Retina-ready on iPhone, Android, tablets, and desktop.

Overall Goal: The map should feel luxurious, modern, uncluttered, and instantly recognizable as a high-end ride-hailing navigation experience comparable to Uber, Bolt, Apple Maps, and Lyft.
