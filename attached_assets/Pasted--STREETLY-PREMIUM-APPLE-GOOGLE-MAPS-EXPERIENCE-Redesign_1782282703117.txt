# STREETLY PREMIUM APPLE + GOOGLE MAPS EXPERIENCE

Redesign Streetly into a world-class platform that combines:

* Apple's premium elegance
* Google Maps' immersive map experience
* Airbnb's usability
* Stripe's clean design
* Arc Browser's futuristic feel

Streetly should NOT feel like a traditional business directory.

Streetly should feel like a map-first technology platform.

The first impression should immediately communicate:

"Explore your city visually."

---

# CORE DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

The map is the product.

The website should feel similar to opening Google Maps for the first time.

Users should immediately see a large immersive map and begin exploring.

The search experience should be central to the entire platform.

Streetly should be:

* Map-first
* Mobile-first
* Search-first
* Discovery-first

---

# HOMEPAGE LAYOUT

## SECTION 1 — IMMERSIVE FULLSCREEN MAP HERO

The mobile experience should feel like a native Google Maps application.

# FINAL OBJECTIVE

Create a platform where users immediately land on a giant interactive map and begin exploring businesses, streets, properties, and opportunities.

The map must be the hero.

The search bar must be the centerpiece.

The design must feel like Apple designed Google Maps for business discovery.

Build all pages, components, animations, map interactions, search systems, filters, dashboards, and responsive experiences required to achieve this vision.
