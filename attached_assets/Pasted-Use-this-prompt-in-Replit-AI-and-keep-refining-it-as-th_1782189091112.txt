# Build Streetly – The Digital Directory of Every Street

Create a modern, mobile-first web application called **Streetly**.

### Project Vision

Streetly is a street-by-street business discovery platform that helps users find businesses around them while enabling field agents to earn money by registering and verifying businesses.

The platform should focus on Nigeria initially but be scalable to Africa.

---

## User Types

### 1. Visitor

Can:

* Search businesses
* Browse by street, district, city, and category
* View business profiles
* View photos
* View business location on a map
* Contact businesses

### 2. Business Owner

Can:

* Register an account
* Claim or create a business listing
* Upload photos
* Edit business information
* View profile analytics
* Upgrade to premium plans

### 3. Field Agent

Can:

* Register and get approved
* Add new businesses
* Upload storefront photos
* Capture GPS location
* Earn commissions for approved listings
* Track earnings
* Request withdrawals

### 4. Admin

Can:

* Approve businesses
* Approve field agents
* Review uploaded photos
* Prevent duplicates
* Manage payments
* View platform statistics
